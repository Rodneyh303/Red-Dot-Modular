# dot.modular knob library

Generated by `panel_src/gen_knobs.py`. **Do not hand-edit** — regenerate instead.

`RDM_<Palette>_<Size>_<Style>.svg` — 4 palettes x 6 sizes x 5 styles = 120.

## Palettes — chosen by measurement, not taste

| | vs dark panel `#232323` | vs light panel `#e6e6e6` | role |
|---|---|---|---|
| `OffWhite` | **12.8:1** | 1.0:1 | hero, dark theme |
| `Dark` | 1.2:1 | **10.9:1** | hero, light theme |
| `Grey` | **4.5:1** | **2.8:1** | secondary, **both** themes |
| `Cream` | 12.3:1 | 1.1:1 | previous hero; kept until the bindings move off it |

`Grey` reads as a control on either panel, so one grey serves both. The dark secondaries
bound today (BPM/LEN/OFFSET, MIX R/M) sit at **1.2:1** against the dark panel — not
subordinate, camouflaged.

`OffWhite` came from sampling the meloDICER photo: its big faces mean `#d8d1c2`, warmth
(R−B) **22** — against our Cream's **23**. Ours was never warmer than the reference; it is
flat. The photo reads white because of a specular highlight we cannot bake (see below), so
`OffWhite` cuts warmth to **8** instead of faking light.

## Sizes — footprints are load-bearing

| Size | viewBox | px | body R | arc gap |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Large | 27.0 | 79.724 | 10.80 | 3.30 |
| Medium | 22.0 | 64.961 | 8.30 | 3.30 |
| Mid | 21.0 | 62.010 | 6.50 | 4.60 |
| Compact | 18.0 | 53.150 | 5.00 | 4.60 |
| Small | 16.0 | 47.240 | 4.00 | 4.60 |
| Trim | 11.0 | 32.480 | 3.60 | — |

`SvgKnob` sizes its box from the SVG, and `MonsoonWidget` computes the mod arc from that box
(`min(box)*radiusFrac + 0.6mm`). So the box pins the arc and **the body radius alone sets the
gap**. Changing either silently moves every arc.

## Styles

| | |
|---|---|
| `Cog` | hero / value. Round rim, raised 6-point cog inset from it, one concave arc between points. |
| `Bar` | **attenuverter** — cog body, pointer as a bar *through* centre. Says bipolar before the angle is read. |
| `Slot` | trim concept. Screwdriver slot; survives the downsample, and is already the bipolar bar. |
| `Quad` | trim concept. 4 flutes: ~2.7px per feature at actual size vs Cog's ~1.8. |
| `Dot` | trim concept. Red dot indicator — most downsample-robust; but ~38 of them may dilute the brand accent. |

## Constraints (why it looks the way it does)

- **Rack rotates the whole SVG.** Geometry is fine (a cog *should* turn). Radially symmetric
  shading is fine. **Directional light is not** — a baked highlight would spin with the knob,
  which is backwards. A fixed gloss must be drawn by the widget, outside the rotation.
- **nanosvg ignores `<filter>`** entirely — the old assets' `feDropShadow` never once rendered.
- **Rack's `svgDraw` keeps only a gradient's first and last stop**, so 3-stop bodies silently
  lost their middle. Everything here is solid fills.
- **Legibility floors.** A trim is ~10px across at zoom 1. The pointer is floored at 0.45mm
  (~1.3px) — it used to scale linearly with R and went **sub-pixel** on Small (0.9px), which is
  what BPM/LEN/OFFSET and MIX R/M use. Strokes scale at `0.028*R` rather than a fixed 0.30mm,
  which was 3x proportionally heavier on a trim than on a Large.
